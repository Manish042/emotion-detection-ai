Healthcare Use Case

Face emotion detection in healthcare helps doctors understand patient emotions, pain levels, and mental health conditions more effectively.

It enables better diagnosis and improves patient care by analyzing facial expressions in real-time.

Emotion AI can detect stress, anxiety, depression, and discomfort in patients, even when they are unable to communicate clearly.

It is highly useful in monitoring ICU patients, elderly care, and mental health treatment.

Doctors can use emotion insights to provide personalized treatment and improve overall healthcare outcomes.
