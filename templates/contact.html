Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response? ▼
Our support team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 4 hours during working hours.
Do you offer a free trial of the API? ▼
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with up to 1,000 API calls. No credit card required. Sign up and get your API key instantly from the dashboard.
Can I integrate Emotion Detection into my existing app? ▼
Absolutely. Our REST API is framework-agnostic and works with Python, JavaScript, Java, Flutter, and more. Full SDK documentation is available in our API section.
Is my data secure and private? ▼
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are GDPR compliant and do not store facial data beyond the processing window unless explicitly configured.
Do you offer enterprise or custom pricing? ▼
Yes. For large-scale deployments, we offer custom pricing, dedicated infrastructure, SLA guarantees, and on-premise deployment options. Contact our business team for a quote.
