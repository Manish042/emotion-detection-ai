Airport & Border Control

Airports handle millions of passengers daily. EmotionAI scans faces in real-time to flag unusual emotional patterns — helping agents focus attention where it matters most.

  • Passive scanning without passenger interruption
  • Integration with existing biometric gates
  • Multi-face tracking in crowded terminals
  • GDPR-compliant data handling

Corporate & Campus Security

Protect offices, data centres, and university campuses with intelligent access control powered by facial emotion analysis. Only calm, authorised individuals pass through — automatically.

  • Tailgating and unauthorised access prevention
  • Emotion-aware entry gates
  • Visitor management with live monitoring
  • 24/7 unattended operation

Public Safety & Crowd Management

Large events — concerts, rallies, stadiums — are challenging to monitor manually. EmotionAI provides a live emotional pulse of the crowd, helping prevent stampedes and disturbances before they start.

  • Crowd density + emotion overlay on maps
  • Early panic or unrest detection
  • Automatic zone-based alert escalation
  • Post-event incident review reports
