Student Engagement

Student engagement refers to the level of attention, curiosity, interest, and involvement that students show during learning activities.

Using face emotion detection, teachers can analyze whether students are focused, bored, confused, or actively participating in the class.

This technology helps improve teaching strategies by providing real-time feedback on student reactions and emotional responses.

In online learning environments, it becomes even more useful as it allows teachers to monitor engagement remotely.
