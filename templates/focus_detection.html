Focus Detection

Focus detection helps analyze whether students are paying attention during lectures or getting distracted. Using AI-based face emotion detection, it becomes possible to track concentration levels in real-time.

This technology identifies signs of boredom, confusion, and lack of engagement through facial expressions and eye movement patterns.

Teachers can use this data to adjust their teaching methods, making lessons more interactive and effective.

In online classes, focus detection ensures that students remain attentive even without physical supervision.
