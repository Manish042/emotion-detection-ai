🚀 The Beginning

Emotion Detection was founded by a team of AI researchers and engineers with a vision to make emotional intelligence accessible to everyone through technology. The idea began in a university lab in Bengaluru, India.

Founding
2020 — First Model

🧠 DeepFace Integration

We launched our first working prototype using DeepFace and OpenCV capable of detecting 7 core emotions — happy, sad, angry, neutral, fear, surprise, and disgust — with 91% accuracy on benchmark datasets.

AI Milestone
2021 — Public Beta

🌐 Web Platform Launch

Launched the public beta of our web platform with image upload and live webcam detection. Within 3 months, over 10,000 users signed up and performed 500,000+ emotion detections.

500K Detections

Released our REST API allowing developers to integrate emotion detection into their own applications. Within 6 months, 200+ companies integrated our API including EdTech, Healthcare, and Security sectors.
