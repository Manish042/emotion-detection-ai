Smart Learning

Smart learning uses artificial intelligence and emotion detection technology to create a more personalized and effective educational experience.

By analyzing facial expressions and student emotions, AI systems can identify whether learners are focused, confused, bored, or actively engaged during lessons.

Teachers can use this information to improve teaching methods, provide better support, and make classroom sessions more interactive.

Smart learning is especially useful in online education platforms where monitoring student participation becomes difficult.

This technology helps improve student performance, attention levels, and overall learning outcomes through real-time emotional analysis.
